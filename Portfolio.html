About

I'm a researcher in motion capture and human–computer interaction, currently finishing a Master's at Universiti Sultan Zainal Abidin (UniSZA). My thesis integrates motion capture data with Gesture Description Language to build motion templates for evaluating football training — essentially, teaching software to recognise good technique the way a coach would.

Before research, I spent two years as a 3D generalist in production — modelling, animating, and rendering scenes from storyboards. That background still shapes how I think about movement, virtual environments, and design today.

I like working end-to-end: capturing the data, writing the code that makes sense of it, and designing the interface or experience that puts it in someone's hands.
